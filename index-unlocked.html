What is your hair porosity?

Try the float test — drop a clean shed strand in a glass of water and watch for 2 to 4 minutes.

Low porosity — strand floats
Water beads on hair, takes forever to get wet, products sit on top rather than soaking in.
Normal porosity — strand sinks slowly
Absorbs and holds moisture well. Hair responds predictably to most products.
High porosity — strand sinks quickly
Absorbs moisture fast but loses it just as fast. Often from color, heat, or chemical services.

Medium density — some scalp visible

Thick or high density — hard to see scalp
Lots of hair. Hard to see scalp even when parted. Hair feels heavy and full.
